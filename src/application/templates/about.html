About

Here at Yum Yuk we are passionate about your food horizons. We use the preferences of people to create food profiles of all the food our customers like, and then compare.

Based on your similarity with that of other user profiles we reccomend foods that you may have never tried. If you haven't signed up already, sign in on our homepage and we'll get you started on expanding your food horizons.
